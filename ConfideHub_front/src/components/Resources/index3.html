Emergency Support

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger or having thoughts of suicide, please reach out to one of these emergency resources immediately:

Service Contact Information Hours Description
National Suicide Prevention Lifeline
 1-800-273-8255 24/7 Provides free and confidential support for people in distress.
Crisis Text Line
 Text HOME to 741741 24/7 Text-based crisis support.
Tanzania Mental Health Association
 +255-22-2118494 8am - 5pm Mental health support services in Tanzania.
Trevor Project (LGBTQ+)
 1-866-488-7386 24/7 Crisis intervention and suicide prevention for LGBTQ+ young people.
National Domestic Violence Hotline
 1-800-799-7233 24/7 Support for anyone experiencing domestic violence.

How to Help Someone in Crisis

  • Stay with them if possible
  • Remove any means of self-harm
  • Listen without judgment
  • Call emergency services if needed
  • Follow up and continue providing support

Warning Signs

  • Talking about wanting to die
  • Looking for ways to end their life
  • Talking about feeling hopeless or having no purpose
  • Increased substance use
  • Withdrawal from activities and loved ones

Safety Planning

  • Identify personal warning signs
  • Create a list of coping strategies
  • Have emergency contacts readily available
  • Make your environment safe
  • Know professional resources you can contact
