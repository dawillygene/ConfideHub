Emergency Support

If you or someone you know is in immediate danger or needs urgent help, please contact these emergency services:

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

1-800-273-8255

Available 24/7

Crisis Text Line

Text HOME to 741741

Available 24/7

Tanzania Mental Health Association

+255 222 113 039

8am - 5pm, Mon-Fri

Important Note

In life-threatening emergencies, always call your local emergency number (911 in US, 112 in EU, 999/112 in Tanzania) immediately.
